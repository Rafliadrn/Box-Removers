The fast, simple way to recycle

More people than ever choose the convenience of online shopping and buying in bulk, but the resulting piles of cardboard boxes cause quite the headache.

You could break them down and shove them in your trashcan, sending them on their way to the landfill. You could pay up to $25 per month to utilize your city’s or town’s curbside recycling program. Or, you could let the boxes take over that corner of your garage or pantry until you have enough free time to load up your car and make the drive to your local recycling facility.

But we offer an alternative – BoxRemovers picks up your cardboard and recycles it for you, all for only $5 per collection service.

How does it work?

BoxRemovers makes recycling a breeze – just sign up, indicate where you want to leave your boxes, and our crew will handle the rest. We’ll pick up your cardboard waste whenever you need us to, so you never have to worry about it again.

We take the boxes to a local recycling facility that recycles cardboard as close to 100% as possible. After your first free pickup, our service costs just $5 per collection. It’s quick, useful, and cheap – a rare combination!
